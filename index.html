Box Styles

Boxes shall be styled in a fashion similar to this with the following guidelines:

  • color: Dawn Pink
  • text-align: center
  • background-color: complementary to font color
  • padding: 5px
  • border: Hippie Pink
  • line-height: 1.4em
  • max-width: 500px
  • border-radius: between 5 and 10px
  • margin: 0 auto
